About Us

Prometheus School has been founded under the aegis of Gauri Shiksha Foundation with an aim to provide holistic education to the children, encompassing – academics, co-curricular activities, sports education and life skills learning. We believe that education is the basis of all progress and entire purpose of education is to inculcate humanitarian values like wisdom, compassion, courage, humility, integrity and reliability in a child. Our mission is to create individuals who grow up as disciplined, responsible, productive citizens of a democratic country and are confident of their potential, goal oriented, sensitive to their environment and above all, co-creators of their own destiny
